About Me

I am Mahnaz Khorsand, born in 1988, with a Master's degree in Architectural Engineering Technology and several years of experience in the challenging yet fascinating path of financial markets.

Since 2019, I became familiar with trading. Like many others, my path was full of confusion, loss, effort and learning. But I never stopped. Now, after years of experience, trial and error, and continuous learning, I'm happy to say that I've been consistently profitable in the Forex market for over three years.

I established this platform to provide training that is not merely theoretical, but based on real experience and applicable in real trading. Simple, practical training without dependency on complex indicators and methods; because I believe simplifying the path is the key to success.

My goal is to transform you into an independent trader, someone who understands the market and makes decisions based on their own analysis - not based on others' signals.

If you're looking for real education, the right path and continuous growth in the world of Forex, you've come to the right place.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is this course suitable for beginners?

Yes. The free course is designed specifically for those who have no prior knowledge of the Forex market.

2. How are these courses different from YouTube and Instagram training?

These courses are structured, step-by-step, project-based and designed with exercises and support. Free content on social media platforms lacks the depth needed for real profitability.

3. Is there mentoring or coaching available after completing the course?

Yes. Students of the professional course can participate in exclusive coaching sessions.

4. Do I need any specific software or tools?

No, all you need is internet, mobile or computer, and motivation for continuous learning and practice.

5. Is a course completion certificate provided?

Currently, we don't provide official certificates, as the main focus is on real skills and practical mastery of the market.
